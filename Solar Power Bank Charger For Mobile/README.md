# ☀️ Solar Power Bank Charger for Mobile

## 📖 Overview

The **Solar Power Bank Charger for Mobile** is a portable and eco-friendly charging solution that utilizes solar energy to charge mobile devices. The system captures solar energy using a photovoltaic (PV) panel, stores it in a rechargeable 3.7V 18650 lithium-ion battery, and delivers a regulated 5V USB output for charging smartphones and other portable electronic devices.

A **134N3P C-Type USB Power Bank Module** with an integrated Battery Management System (BMS) ensures safe battery charging, voltage regulation, and protection against overcharging, over-discharging, overcurrent, and short circuits. The project demonstrates the practical application of renewable energy for portable power solutions.

---

## ⚙️ Working Principle

1. The **5V mini solar panel** converts sunlight into DC electrical energy.
2. The generated power is supplied to the **134N3P USB Power Bank Module**.
3. The module safely charges the **3.7V 18650 lithium-ion battery** while protecting it using the built-in BMS.
4. When a mobile device is connected, the module boosts the battery voltage to a stable **5V USB output**.
5. The stored energy is used to charge smartphones and other USB-powered devices even when sunlight is unavailable.
